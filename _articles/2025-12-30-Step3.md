## Goal Setting and Action Workbook - 2026

### Step 3: Define Goals

*“If you have built castles in the sky, let not your dreams go to waste. Just build the foundations under them.” ~ Henry David Thoreau*

List the core areas of your life where you want to get results. Examples include: Health, Continued Learning, Helping Others, Marriage, Business. The more creative you can get with your naming, the more committed you’ll be (i.e. Expand the Mind instead of Continued Learning).
