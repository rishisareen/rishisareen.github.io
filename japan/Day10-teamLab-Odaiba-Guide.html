🌧️ Why Today Beat Hakone

This day was picked over a Hakone/Fuji trip on purpose: in tsuyu (June rainy season) Mt Fuji hides in cloud most days, and a 2-hour trek to a view you can't see is a gamble. teamLab Planets is fully indoor and a guaranteed hit at 17 & 12 — the weather-proof pick.

1
Book teamLab Planets TIMED TICKETS — well ahead
Official site or Klook · pick the ~10:30 slot
Sunday slots sell out days in advance. Entry is by timed slot — book on the official site or Klook, ~¥3,800–4,200 per adult, and all three of you pay adult price. Do this the moment you read this page.

Getting there: Tamachi → Shin-Toyosu
Yamanote → Shimbashi → Yurikamome line
Yamanote Line from Tamachi to Shimbashi, then switch to the Yurikamome — an automated, elevated, driverless line. Grab the front-row seats for great views sweeping over the bay. Ride to Shin-Toyosu; teamLab Planets is right there.
Front seats!~35–40 min door to door

🗓️ The Day, Hour by Hour

One booked anchor in the morning, then a free-flowing Odaiba afternoon — easy to stretch or trim depending on energy and weather.

🚆 ~09:30 · Head out
Tamachi → Toyosu
Yamanote to Shimbashi, then the Yurikamome automated elevated line — sit in the front seats (it's driverless, so you get the windscreen view over the bay) — to Shin-Toyosu. teamLab Planets is right outside the station.
🌊 ~10:30 · The anchor
teamLab Planets — book this slot
Barefoot museum: you wade through knee-deep water in one exhibit, so shorts or rollable trousers (lockers + towels provided). Mirror floors = skirts not ideal. Allow ~2 hrs: the Water Area + the Garden Area with its floating orchids. Phones allowed — best photos of the trip.
🍣 ~13:00 · Lunch
Toyosu — two easy options
Option A: Toyosu Senkyaku Banrai — Edo-style onsen/food complex near the market, great sushi. Option B: hop the Yurikamome to Odaiba early and eat at the DiverCity food court.
🤖 ~14:30 · Odaiba
Gundam, shopping & the bay
Yurikamome to Odaiba: the life-size Unicorn Gundam outside DiverCity (it transforms/lights up at scheduled times — check the board), DiverCity shopping for the teens, Odaiba beach with the Rainbow Bridge view, and the Statue of Liberty replica. If the weather turns foul: the Miraikan science museum is the indoor backup.
🌉 ~17:30 · Optional finale
Rainbow Bridge + Tokyo Tower light up
If everyone still has gas: stay on the Odaiba waterfront to watch the Rainbow Bridge and Tokyo Tower light up across the water, then Yurikamome → Shimbashi → Yamanote back to Tamachi.
🍜 Evening · Decide Day 11
Easy dinner near Tamachi + the big decision
Tomorrow is the last full day: DisneySea OR a Harajuku/Shinjuku city loop. Decide tonight — and if DisneySea wins, buy the tickets tonight (they're date-locked).
