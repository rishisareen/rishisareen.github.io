🚇 Getting there

From Tamachi (Pullman): Toei Asakusa line from Mita Stn → Daimon → Toei Oedo line to Toshimaen Station (~50–60 min; or JR Yamanote to Ikebukuro + Seibu line). Short walk to the Studio. Arrive ~20 min before your timed slot.

🗺️ Suggested Flow (~4–5 hrs)

Your timed slot
Great Hall → core sets
Tour starts with the Great Hall reveal, then self-paced: Dumbledore's office, Gryffindor common room, moving staircases.
Midway
Diagon Alley + Butterbeer break
Explore Diagon Alley, then pause at the Backlot Café for Butterbeer before the second half.
Second half
Forbidden Forest → Platform 9¾ → Tokyo-exclusives
Forbidden Forest, Hogwarts Express, then the Tokyo-only Ministry of Magic and Quidditch green-screen.
Finale
Hogwarts model → gift shop
End at the giant castle model, then the gift shop. Total ~4–5 hrs on your feet.
After
Ikebukuro
Toshimaen is close to Ikebukuro — easy afternoon stop for food & shopping on the way back.

💡 Tips & Tricks

⏱️
Block out 4–6 hours
It's the world's largest indoor HP facility — rushing wastes it. Don't schedule anything tight afterwards.
👟
Wear comfortable shoes
It's a long time on your feet with few places to sit. Dress for walking.
🎟️
Buy via a free-cancellation reseller
Direct tickets can't be date-changed once booked. A reseller (GetYourGuide/Klook) keeps flexibility — and book before the 1 July price rise.
🕙
Arrive ~20 min before your slot
No refunds for late arrival/no-show. Allow buffer for the 50–60 min journey from Tamachi.
🪄
Decide on extras in advance
Interactive wands and green-screen broomstick photos cost extra — decide so you're not upsold on the spot.
☔
Perfect rainy-day pick
Entirely indoor — ideal if 19 June turns wet during tsuyu (rainy season). Weekday (Fri) also means lighter crowds.
💳
Pay on Magnus Burgundy in JPY
Lowest forex (2%); decline DCC (paying in INR). Stack the Mastercard/Klook offer if it applies.
