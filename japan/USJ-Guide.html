🚆 Getting there

From Namba (Swissôtel): JR to Universal-City Station (~15–20 min, easy). Arrive before opening — the 25th-anniversary crowds are huge this year.

⚠️ Read this first

2026 is USJ's 25th Anniversary + Cool Japan (Detective Conan, Jujutsu Kaisen, Frieren) — crowds are at an all-time high. Two non-negotiables: (1) get a Super Nintendo World Area Timed Entry the instant the park opens (free, in the app) — slots can vanish by 10:30am — or have it bundled in an Express Pass; (2) an Express Pass is essential, not a luxury, this year.

🗺️ Suggested Flow

Before opening
Arrive early + lock Nintendo entry
Be at the gate 30–45 min before open. The moment you're in (or in the app), grab your Super Nintendo World Area Timed Entry — unless your Express Pass already includes it.
Morning
Biggest Express rides first
Hit Flying Dinosaur and Harry Potter Forbidden Journey early with Express, while everyone else queues for Nintendo.
Your Nintendo time slot
Super Nintendo World + Donkey Kong
Mario Kart → Mine-Cart Madness → Yoshi. If you bought Power-Up Bands, play the interactive games. Mobile-order lunch around now.
Afternoon
Hollywood Dream, Cool Japan, shows
Hollywood Dream coaster, the anime limited attractions, Minion Park, and any stage shows. Use indoor rides during peak heat.
Evening
Night parade / show + re-rides
Catch the nighttime entertainment, then re-ride favourites as waits drop near closing.

💡 Tips & Tricks

⚡
Express Pass is essential in 2026
25th-anniversary crowds make it a must, not a luxury. Express Pass 7 is the most balanced — covers the top rides and removes the Nintendo uncertainty. Buy in advance (dynamic pricing).
🎟️
Secure Nintendo timed entry ASAP
Free Area Timed Entry slots are issued in the app and can sell out by ~10:30am. Grab it the second you enter — or skip the worry with an Express Pass that includes it.
📱
The USJ app runs your day
Timed entry, live wait times, mobile food order, show times. Download & set up before you arrive.
🍄
Power-Up Band (optional)
Buy if the kids want the interactive Nintendo games (punch blocks, collect keys). Not needed just to ride.
🧍
Use Single Rider lines
For Flying Dinosaur, Hollywood Dream & others — big time-saver if you don't mind splitting up briefly.
💳
Pay smart on tickets
Route the (priciest) ticket+Express bundle through your Axis Forex card for 25% off via Klook if you have it; otherwise Magnus Burgundy, billed in JPY.
🌧️
Hot & rainy season
Water, sun cover, poncho. Cluster indoor rides (Mario Kart, Forbidden Journey, Minion) at midday.

⚡ Express Pass — which one?

⚡ Express Pass 7
Recommended for 2026
  • Covers 7 rides incl. the highest-demand ones
  • Versions exist that include Super Nintendo World timed entry — pick one of those
  • Removes the biggest uncertainty of the day
👉 Best choice for your visit. Confirm the EP7 variant you buy includes Nintendo Area entry.
⚡ Express Pass 4
Cheaper, lighter
  • Covers 4 rides — fine on a quieter day, but 2026 is not quiet
  • Often still needs you to grab Nintendo entry separately
🆓 Free Area Timed Entry
App lottery — no cost
  • Only way into Super Nintendo World without an Express Pass
  • Sells out fast on busy days — act at park open
