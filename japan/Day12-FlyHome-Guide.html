💡 Worth Knowing

💴
Spend-down yen — but keep the ICOCAs
Burn leftover yen on konbini/airport snacks, or keep coins as souvenirs. ICOCA cards stay valid 10 years — keep them for the next Japan trip. (Refunds are JR West stations only — back in Kansai, not possible in Tokyo — so keeping them is the move.)
🛍️
Tax-free bags in carry-on
Keep sealed tax-free purchases in carry-on if possible — customs can ask to check the sealed bags on exit.
🍫
Save souvenir shopping for T3
Haneda T3 has solid last-minute souvenir shopping airside — Tokyo Banana, KitKats, character goods. Don't overbuy in town; the airport has it all.
⏱️
Watch the clock
International cutoff is ~60 min before departure, but immigration queues at HND T3 mid-morning can run 30+ minutes. Being airside by ~09:00 keeps everything stress-free.
