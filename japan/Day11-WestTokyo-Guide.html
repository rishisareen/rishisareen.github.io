🗓️ The Day, Hour by Hour

A classic west-side loop: forest calm first, teen chaos second, skyline and lantern alleys to finish. Last full day — end it with bags half-packed.

🚆 ~09:00 · Roll out
Yamanote: Tamachi → Harajuku
~20 min on the loop line. Exit at Harajuku — the shrine entrance is literally next to the station.
⛩️ ~09:15 · Meiji Jingu
Forested shrine — calm before the shopping chaos
A genuine forest in the middle of Tokyo, right next to Harajuku station. Walk under the giant torii, past the sake barrel wall, to the main shrine. Free, peaceful, and a perfect contrast to what comes next. ~1 hr loop.
🌈 ~10:30 · Takeshita Street
Harajuku — teen heaven
Crepes (Marion Crepes or Angels Heart), giant rainbow cotton candy, purikura photo booths, thrift & kawaii shops — Riya and Ryan will not want to leave. Then drift down Cat Street toward Omotesando for calmer, designer-lined streets.
🥟 ~12:30 · Lunch
Around Harajuku / Omotesando
Gyoza is the reliable crowd-pleaser here, or pick one of the trendy cafés along Cat Street / Omotesando. Refuel properly — the afternoon is on foot too.
🏙️ ~14:00 · Shinjuku skyline
Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building — FREE deck
Yamanote 2 stops to Shinjuku, then the free observation deck on the 45th floor (202 m) — full city panorama, Mt Fuji on a clear day. Optional green break after: Shinjuku Gyoen (¥500, last entry varies — gates close ~17:30–18:00; June = iris season).
🏮 ~17:00 · Shinjuku evening
Omoide Yokocho → Kabukicho neon → Don Quijote
Omoide Yokocho ("Memory Lane") — tiny lantern-lit yakitori alleys by the station. Atmospheric; go early with kids (smoky and small, but fine pre-7pm). Then the Kabukicho neon walk + the 3D Godzilla head on the Toho building, and Don Quijote for last-night souvenir chaos.
🧳 ~20:00 · Back & PACK
Tamachi — packing + front-desk admin
JL39 leaves Haneda 10:55 tomorrow — leave the Pullman ~07:00–07:30. Tonight: pack everything, and settle Accor points / both room bookings at the front desk so the morning is just checkout-and-go.

💡 Worth Knowing

📅
Monday works in your favour
Lighter crowds everywhere today — Meiji Jingu, Takeshita Street and the observation deck are all noticeably calmer than on a weekend.
🗼
Metro Govt deck — free and open late
Last entry ~22:00, so it also works as an evening backup. One of the two towers is closed on some Mondays — check which tower (North or South) is open today before going up.
💴
Use up loose yen at Don Quijote
Perfect place to burn remaining cash on snacks and souvenirs — and it's tax-free with passports, so bring all three.
⚖️
Bag weigh-in tonight
JL economy is typically 23 kg × 2 pieces each — but check the JAL award baggage allowance on the booking to be sure. Better to shuffle weight tonight than at the Haneda counter.
