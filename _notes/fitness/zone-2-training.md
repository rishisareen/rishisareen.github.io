Zone 2 training is the foundation of endurance performance.

## What is Zone 2?

- 60-70% of max heart rate
- Can hold a conversation
- Feels "easy" but purposeful

## Benefits

- Builds aerobic base
- Improves fat oxidation
- Enhances recovery
- Increases mitochondrial density

## How Much?

80% of training volume should be Zone 2 for endurance athletes.
