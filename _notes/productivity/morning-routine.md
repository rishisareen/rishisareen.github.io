A good morning routine sets the tone for the entire day.

## My Current Routine

1. Wake up at 5:30 AM
2. No phone for first 30 minutes
3. Hydrate - drink water
4. Light stretching or yoga
5. Review daily goals

## Key Principles

- Start slow, don't rush
- Consistency over intensity
- Protect this time fiercely
