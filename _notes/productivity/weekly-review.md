The weekly review is the most important habit for staying on track.

## When

Every Sunday evening, 30-45 minutes.

## Steps

1. **Clear inboxes** - email, notes, tasks
2. **Review calendar** - past week and upcoming
3. **Update projects** - what's the next action?
4. **Reflect** - what went well? What didn't?
5. **Plan** - set intentions for next week

## Tools

- Calendar for time blocks
- Task manager for actions
- Journal for reflection
